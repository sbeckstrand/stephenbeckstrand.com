Welcome to Project Shrines!

There are plenty of resources available for Breath of the Wild including interactive maps and walkthroughs but my goal here is to provide a resource to help locate shrines specifically as well as provide steps on how to find them and how to beat them. Please note that this Project is very much still in progress. At this point it is mostly just the laytout that is finished but intend to slowly add content to it. In the meantime, feel free to reach out to me directly via email with any recommended updates or if you would like to contribute
