Build Using: Javascript

The goal of this application was to help me better familiarize myself with vanilla Javascript and as such, with this project no JS frameworks were used. It initially started out as a Sudoku app but I struggled finding the right algorithm to make the Sudoko applicatio work properly. I was at a fair with my wife and at this fair we participated in their Bingo event. While playing I realized this is something I could make a little online game out of and that it included several different game mades and functions that logically made sense to me.

In this app you have the option to choose between four different game modes; Single, Double, Pictureframe or Blackout

  • Single: Be the first to get a single Bingo (straight line in any direction)
  • Double: Be the first to get two Bingos
  • Pictureframe: Be the first to fill in all of the spaces that outline your bingo card.
  • Blackout: Be the first to fill in all of the spaces on the card

After selecting your game mode you can then include the number of opponents to play against (Anywhere between 1-20)

When playing, each time you press the interactive button at the top, a new number is randomly generated. If one of the numbers is on your card, you will be prompted to select it before you can continue. Once either you or one of your opponents has met the win conditions for the selected gamemode, the game will end and you will be prompted with a message stating if you won or not. While playing, you have the option to choose to see your opponents progress or not. If you and another opponent get the last square at the same time, you will be prompted to select it on your board and you will be considered the winner!
